How to Play

Itâ€™s pretty simple!

You and your opponent are given a list of numbers from 1 to 9. In the list contains four of each number. The object of the game is to get all your numbers crossed out before your opponent.

You do this by clicking the green button to simulate writing a number and then your opponent has to guess what number you wrote. If your opponent guesses the wrong number, one of the numbers you wrote is removed. If your opponent guesses the right number, it is your opponent turn to write numbers and your turn to guess.

The first player to get all their numbers crossed out wins!
